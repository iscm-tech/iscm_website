# Internal Portal User Guide

## 1. Purpose

The internal portal, also referred to as ISCMer, provides authorized internal users with a central hub to access internal information, useful links, regulations, procedures, templates, and group-specific resources.

This guide is intended for instruct members on how to use the portal and does not cover technical administration.

## 2. Target Users

The guide is intended for:

- authorized ISCM internal members;
- new personnel completing onboarding;
- functional-group members;
- member who need internal regulations, procedures, or templates; and
- approved users who need access to UEH and ISCM internal resources.

## 3. Access Requirements

Before accessing the internal portal, users need:

- a web browser;
- an active internet connection;
- access to https://iscm.ueh.edu.vn/iscmer; and
- a UEH email or other approved account, according to the current access policy.

New personnel who do not yet have access must follow the approved registration/access-request process managed by ISCM PR Communication Team.

## 4. Login Instructions

1. Open https://iscm.ueh.edu.vn/iscmer.
2. If redirected to the login page, select **Login with UEH Email**.
3. Sign in through the Google provider flow.
4. Complete any account verification requested by the identity provider.
5. After successful login, the system returns you to the ISCMer Welcome Page.

## 5. Main Information Areas

The available menu may vary by language and current content configuration.

### UEH and related resources

This area provides links to UEH or related systems, including:

- UEHer;
- finance and accounting resources;
- knowledge hub resources;
- training-program search; and
- academic calendar/timeline information.

### ISCM overview

This area may include:

- ISCM strategy and plans;
- ISCM relationships with UEH, CTD, UEH Co-Lab, Convergence Tech Hub, and MakerSpace;
- organizational structure; and
- ISCM evaluation information.

### General governance

This area may include:

- rules and regulations;
- finance and payment guidance;
- internal meeting regulations;
- event organization management;
- equipment management;
- book management;
- templates and documents; and
- storage/data-structure guidance.

### Functional groups

Group-specific resources may be available for:

- Operation and Finance;
- Academia;
- Research;
- Community Engagement;
- PR and Communication; and
- Partnership.

### Onboarding information

The ISCMer landing area may provide onboarding information for new members, including internal systems, work references, personnel groups, and communication channels.

## 6. How to Access Internal Links and Resources

1. Open the ISCMer menu from the website header.
2. Select the relevant information group.
3. Select the required page or external resource link.
4. Read any instructions before opening or downloading a document.
5. If a link opens an external UEH or Google resource, use the account authorized for that resource.
6. Return to ISCMer through the browser or website navigation after completing the task.

Use the portal as the primary internal reference before submitting routine information requests.

## 7. Usage Notes

- Select the correct English or Vietnamese locale when content is available in both languages.
- Internal pages may link to external systems managed by UEH, Google, or another owner.
- External links can have separate access permissions.
- Content availability may depend on your approved account and current portal configuration.
- Check the portal regularly for updated regulations, templates, and internal notices.
- Do not assume an old downloaded copy is still current; verify the latest version on the portal.

## 8. Security Notes

- Do not share your account or login session.
- Do not forward restricted internal links or documents to unauthorized users.
- Sign out when using a shared computer.
- Do not save passwords in public or shared browsers.
- Report suspicious login prompts or unexpected access requests to Technical Team.
- Do not include confidential information in screenshots sent to external support.

## 9. Troubleshooting

### Login page keeps appearing

- Confirm that you are using the approved account.
- Retry login in a private/incognito browser window.
- Clear stale site cookies and retry.
- Confirm that browser pop-ups and third-party login redirects are not blocked.

### Google/UEH login succeeds but access is denied

- Confirm that your account has been approved.
- New personnel may need to complete the access-registration process.
- Contact Technical Team with your name and approved email address. Do not send your password.

### Internal page does not load

- Refresh the page.
- Confirm your internet connection.
- Try the other language only if the content is expected there.
- Check whether other internal pages work.
- Report the affected page title and time of failure to Technical Team.

### External link requests another login

External UEH, Google, or partner resources may require separate permissions. Contact the owner of that resource.

### Information appears outdated

Report the page title, section, and expected correction to Technical Team. Do not edit or redistribute outdated internal copies as official information.
